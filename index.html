About SS600

Redefining Energy-Efficient Mobility

The Simply Silent 600, also referred to as the SS600, is a significantly modified Aptera-inspired vehicle. With solar power, this car can travel 60 kilometers per day. Additionally, it has a 600-kilometer range between charges. This three-seater car will cost you about 13 lakhs. In order to prevent aerodynamic drag, it has both an integrated and a deployable solar panel that will only activate when the vehicle is at rest. An EV charging station is not necessary to charge this transportable solar panel.

Our Technology

Solar Power Integration :

Our solar-powered vehicle features advanced panels that harness sunlight, allowing for up to 40 miles of travel per day without traditional charging. This innovative integration reduces reliance on fossil fuels and promotes a cleaner, sustainable future in transportation.

Battery and range :

Our solar-powered vehicle features advanced battery technology, providing an impressive range of up to 1,000 miles on a single charge. Combined with solar energy generation, it ensures long-lasting performance and efficiency for all your journeys.

Aerodynamics :

Our vehicle's sleek, three-wheeled design minimizes drag, enhancing efficiency and performance. This innovative aerodynamic shape allows for smooth airflow, reducing resistance and maximizing the benefits of solar power, ensuring a superior driving experience.
